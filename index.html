About The Band

Midnight Echoes is an alternative rock band formed in 2015 in Los Angeles, California. Known for their haunting melodies and introspective lyrics, the band has quickly risen to prominence in the indie music scene.

The band consists of four members who met while studying music at UCLA. Their unique sound blends elements of post-punk, dream pop, and shoegaze, creating a sonic landscape that's both nostalgic and forward-thinking.

With three studio albums and numerous international tours under their belt, Midnight Echoes continues to push boundaries and captivate audiences worldwide.
